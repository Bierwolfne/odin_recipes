Butter Chicken

Ingredients:

  • 1 lb boneless chicken thighs, cut into bite-sized pieces
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 2 tbsp lemon juice
  • 1 tbsp garam masala
  • 2 tsp ground cumin
  • 1 tsp ground turmeric
  • 1/2 tsp chili powder
  • 2 tbsp butter
  • 1 large onion, finely chopped
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 1 tbsp fresh ginger, minced
  • 1 can (14 oz) tomato puree
  • 1 cup heavy cream
  • 1/4 cup fresh cilantro, chopped (for garnish)
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • Cooked basmati rice (for serving)

Instructions:

  1. In a bowl, combine the chicken pieces, yogurt, lemon juice, garam masala, cumin, turmeric, and chili powder. Let the chicken marinate for at least 30 minutes, preferably 1-2 hours for better flavor.
  2. Heat butter in a large pan over medium heat. Add the chopped onions and saut√© until they are soft and golden brown.
  3. Add the garlic and ginger, and cook for another minute until fragrant.
  4. Stir in the tomato puree, reduce the heat, and simmer for about 10 minutes until the sauce thickens slightly.
  5. Add the marinated chicken to the pan, along with any remaining marinade. Cook for 10-12 minutes until the chicken is fully cooked.
  6. Pour in the heavy cream and stir well. Simmer for an additional 5-10 minutes until the sauce is rich and creamy. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  7. Garnish with fresh cilantro and serve with warm basmati rice.
