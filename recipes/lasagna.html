Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients:

  • 1 pound ground beef
  • 1/2 pound Italian sausage
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 2 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cans (14.5 ounces) diced tomatoes
  • 2 cans (6 ounces) tomato paste
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons dried basil
  • 1 teaspoon Italian seasoning
  • 12 lasagna noodles, cooked and drained
  • 2 cups ricotta cheese
  • 3 cups mozzarella cheese, shredded
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. In a large skillet, cook the ground beef, sausage, onion, and garlic over medium heat until browned. Drain any excess fat.
  2. Stir in the diced tomatoes, tomato paste, sugar, basil, Italian seasoning, and salt. Simmer for 30 minutes, stirring occasionally.
  3. Preheat your oven to 350°F (175°C). Spread a thin layer of the meat sauce at the bottom of a 9x13 inch baking dish.
  4. Layer noodles, ricotta, mozzarella, Parmesan, and sauce in the dish, repeating layers until all ingredients are used.
  5. Cover with aluminum foil and bake for 45 minutes. Remove foil and bake for an additional 10 minutes, until the cheese is bubbly and golden.
  6. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  7. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
