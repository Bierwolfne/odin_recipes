World's Best Lasagna

Description

Making lasagna can be time-consuming, but the results are well worth the wait. You'll find a detailed ingredient list and step-by-step instructions in the recipe below:

Ingredients:

  • Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Spices and Seasonings
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses

Steps:

  1. Put sauce in the pan.
  2. Light the pan on the stove and cook the sauce until it simmers.
  3. Layer noodles, sauce, and cheese in a baking dish.
  4. Bake at 350°F (175°C) for 45 minutes.
  5. Let the lasagna cool for 10 minutes before serving.
  6. Enjoy your delicious lasagna!
