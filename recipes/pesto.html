Pesto Pasta

Ingredients:

  • 2 cups fresh basil leaves
  • 1/2 cup grated Parmesan cheese
  • 1/2 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/3 cup pine nuts
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste
  • 1 lb pasta (your choice)

Instructions:

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Cook the pasta according to the package instructions until al dente. Drain and set aside.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, prepare the pesto sauce. In a food processor or blender, combine the basil leaves, Parmesan cheese, pine nuts, and garlic.
  3. Pulse the mixture until it forms a coarse paste.
  4. Slowly add the olive oil while the processor is running, blending until the pesto is smooth. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. In a large mixing bowl, toss the cooked pasta with the pesto sauce until evenly coated.
  6. Serve the pesto pasta with extra Parmesan cheese and fresh basil, if desired. Enjoy!
